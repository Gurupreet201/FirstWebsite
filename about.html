Gymnasium

Medi-Caps Gymnasium being clean and airy has a good selection of free-weights, resistance machines and good equipments. A gym can have all the atmosphere and ambience in the world, but if it does not have good facilities it will not be worth much. Hostel

The University has magnificently designed separate hostels for boys and girls each with all modern facilities for boarding and other recreational activities. The hostel buildings are remarkable pieces of architecture. Carefully designed structure the temperature inside the buildings remain comfortable even in extremely hot conditions. Twenty-four hours security assured. Hostel committee, a student body, manages the hostel. The Hostel committee in consultation with students takes care of different aspects like hygiene, discipline etc.

Transport Facility

The Group has a fleet of more than 29 buses for the convenience of the students and its staff members. Besides, the solid web of local buses makes it convenient for any person from Indore, Ujjain or MHOW to commute to the Institute. The group provides the facility of safe travelling through its in-house shuttle buses with various pick-up and drop-off points in the city of Indore and its outskirts.
